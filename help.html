Desk Planner – User Guide

Overview

The Desk Planner allows you to assign desks to teams across the working week using an interactive floor plan. You can import team lists, allocate desks visually, and export your plan for reuse.

---

Basic Workflow

  • Select a day from the dropdown
  • Click one or more desks on the floor plan
  • Click a team in the legend to assign
  • Repeat for other days as needed
Tip: Hold Ctrl or enable Multi to select multiple desks at once.
---

Teams

Import Teams

Use the Import Teams button to load a CSV file with team IDs and names.

Example format:

team_id,team_name
T1,Finance
T2,HR
T3,IT

Add Team

Use Add Team to create a new team on the fly. The system will automatically assign the next available team ID (e.g. T1, T4, etc).

---

Assigning Desks

  • Select desks by clicking on the map
  • Click a team in the legend to assign
  • Click empty space to clear selection
---

Copy Between Days

Use the Copy button to copy desk assignments from the current day to one or more other days.

---

Import / Export

Export

Export saves:

  • Desk assignments for each day
  • Team IDs (T1, T2, etc)
  • Team names

Import

Import restores:

  • Desk assignments
  • Team names (automatically rebuilds the legend)
You do not need to re-import the team list if using an exported file.
---

Legend

The legend shows all available teams and their colours. Clicking a team assigns it to the selected desks.

---

Summary

The summary shows:

  • Total desks
  • Assigned vs free desks
  • Desk count per team
---

Compare Mode

Switch to Compare mode to view two days side by side. Editing is disabled in this mode.

---

Other Features

  • Undo – revert last change
  • Reset – clear all data
  • Multi – toggle multi-select mode
---

Tips

  • Use CSV export regularly to save your work
  • Keep team IDs consistent across floors
  • Use Compare mode to review occupancy changes
